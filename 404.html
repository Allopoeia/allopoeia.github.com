Suddenly, everything vanishes…

You find yourself in some abyss. You strain yourself to hear something… anything… Not a pip. There's no one here but yourself — you can almost feel the absence of things. All you are certain of is the coldness of this place… Wait, what's this!? A stack of lightly-toasted waffles at your side. Alas, they won't last long — the void itself is draining your energy. “I've gotta get out of this place,” you murmur.

Whilst nibbling on the waffles, you search the area. The ground – if you could call it that – is unlike any material your fingers have felt before. There is no wind; everything is stagnant; you can only smell yourself and the waffles.

Another object! It seems to be a large towel. You wrap it around yourself, attempting to fend off the bitter cold. More nibbling.

After stumbling about some more, you come upon a large… something — you decide against trying to christen it. It is seemingly gargantuan, and hard as stone; its surface is smooth, yet strewn with jagged facets. “How bizarre…” Feeling around the something, it seems to be blocking some kind of opening.

“Maybe I can push it aside…” Nibble, nibble– scarfing down the remainders. Folding the towel around your upper arm for protection, you prop yourself against the side of the facety something.

Push! Puuush! Puu– falling!

Flood of light! You find yourself in a painfully bright room, seated in a chair in front of an unfamiliar wall-mounted digital screen.

Rub eyes. … “Where am I…?”

Ding! The screen draws your attention. Text appears; it reads:

Greetings, #404! If you're reading this, you've just fatefully passed through – don't panic! – Bakgannon's Wormhole. The fact that you're reading this right now means you're perfectly fine!

Link up to E-H-4421-0-79#KM with tag BKG-n8 for debriefing. Also, since you're in node 8, make sure you flush the system before you do anything (that series 7 tech is unstable with stacking). Also also, diagbots should be running scans to ensure you aren't contaminated — purely a precaution; in linteo confidimus!

P.S. There have been reported sightings of “waffles” (a mass-produced foodstuff from DC 20) in the BKG. If you come across them, don't even look at them funny — they are incredibly potent. If you happen to have ignored all reason by eating the waffles, get yourself into isolation immediately — the diagbots can't get a reading on them for some reason.

— K. M., 2192-13-24

You are left bewildered, and absolutely covered in crumbs. To your surprise, the towel is still with you, and incredibly clean.

Nothing changes. No ‘diagbots’, and no change on the screen…
