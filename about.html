Allopoeia (or, hypocoristically, The Allo) is an open-authorship, all-encompassing journal/blag created by plash. It is powered by jekyll and a copious supply of waffles. Its raw data can be found on GitHub, where the production site is also currently served.

The Allo is open to any persons wishing to express things of all kinds — political opinion, bashing big corporations, a poem about your pet gerbil, review, preview, postview, proview, antiview, story, history, criticism, witticism, felid study, etc. etc. — permitting we like said persons or find whatever they wish to share interesting and/or amusing.
